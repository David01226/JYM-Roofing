Services

Roofing

At JYM we offer a range of Roofing services for both pitched and flat roofs. We offer full reroof services and have experience working with various roof coverings. Whether it be slate, tile or stone roof coverings can be replaced as well as replacing existing felt with a new breathable membrane system, solving any condensation issues. We also offer full flat roof replacement surfaces along with a range of flat roof repair options.

We also offer a range of roof repairs. These include changing broken tiles, re-pointing of ridge and hip lines, replacement of lead flashings and soakers, sealing of cracks and joints making the roof watertight.

Our services also include restoration of existing guttering and replacement of cast iron, wooden and UPVC guttering and downpipes.

Our team are also Dry Verge/Ridge installation specialists. Providing an effective watertight system that eliminates the need for mortar whilst securely fixing interlocking tiles.

Pointing

At JYM we offer a full range of pointing services for all kinds of construction and property maintenance related issues. We can offer full repointing of brickwork and stonework Walls, chimneys, gables and paving.

Cladding

We can install cladding of various styles and materials including wood, UPVC or composite. At JYM we specialise in the installation of composite cladding which provides enhanced durability and aesthetic appeal.

Tree Surgery and Lawn Care

At JYM our specialist is fully ticketed for chainsaw operation and tree felling and is ready to assist with any tree or hedge removal or pruning. We also offer Ivy removal from walls and roof surfaces.

We can also offer a lawn treatment plan which includes weed and moss treatments along with aeration as required to promote a green healthy lawn.
